Metadata (EN)

Title: Improve the quality of life of people with Autism Spectrum Disorder through workplace experience: an investigation in Lombardy

Abstract: Individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorder face significant challenges enteringthe workforce; yet research in this area is limited and the issues arepoorly understood. This research investigates the work experience of 20 individualsfrom job search to confirmation within the organization. Throughinterviews with individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorder, family members,company tutors and supported placement counselors, this study highlightedthe benefits to the quality of life of the subjects and the strategiesthat can accomplish a positive experience.

Title: Améliorer la qualité de vie des personnes atteintes de troubles du spectre autistique grâce à une expérience de travail: une enquête sur le territoire de Lombardie
