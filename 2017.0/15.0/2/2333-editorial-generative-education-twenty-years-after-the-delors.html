Title: Editorial: Generative Education: twenty years after the Delors report

Abstract: With the UNESCO Learning report: The Treasure Within (1996) Jacques Delors entrusted to the 21st century school the task of promoting four fundamental types of learning, interconnected from each other, defining them as the four pillars on which to base the educational action: 1. Learn to know;2. Learn to do;3. Learn to be, 4. Learn to live together.
