Title: The factors that improve an active aging process: an investigation in northern Italy

Abstract: The recent demographically change, characterized by lengthening of theaverage life and consequently the gradual aging of the population, has ledto a growing interest regarding the role and the position taken by elder intoday’s society. This interest has directed numerous studies aimed at identifyinga number of factors that enable the maintenance of a state of wellbeingand development of the potential of each individual.In this direction we conducted a research with the aim to identify the factorsfor a process of active aging. Such research has leading 32 subjects,who underwent an interview and a cognitive test (MMSE). The resultsshowed the existence of three factors: autonomy and ability to make decisions,the ability to remain active and committed, and the quality of the relationshipsand the support of others.
