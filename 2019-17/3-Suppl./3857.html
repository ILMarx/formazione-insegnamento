Metadata (EN)

Title: The end of play. The disappearance of the free play and its effects on children’s wellbeing

Abstract: The disappearance of childhood and the adultification of play are two issues that are occurring in the modern societies, with negative effects on children’s well-being. The paper seeks to systematize the scientific literature concerning these themes and to propose a possible classification for the different types of play. A proposal for a cultural and social change is also presented by taking into account the key role of the school in this area.

Title: Fine dei giochi. La scomparsa del gioco libero e le sue ricadute sul benessere dei bambini e delle bambine

Abstract: Scomparsa dell’infanzia e gioco adultizzato sono due dinamiche che stanno interessando le moderne società, con effetti negativi sul benessere dei bambini e delle bambine.
