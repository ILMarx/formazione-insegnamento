Metadata (EN)

Title: Dostoevsky's reflection on education: The Last letters

Abstract: The article focuses on the pedagogical insights offered by Fyodor Dostoevsky’s Last letters, to be correlated with the narrative production. The result is a subtle sensitivity towards childhood and adolescence, whose specific problems stand out against the background of the European value crisis of the late Nineteenth century.

Keywords: Christian education; Condition of childhood in Eastern Europe; Crisis of European culture; Dostoevsky's Last Letters; Russian student movements of the Nineteenth century
