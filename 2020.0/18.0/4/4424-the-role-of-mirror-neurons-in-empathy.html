Metadata (EN)

Title: The role of mirror neurons in empathy

Abstract: The article will focus on revising based on recent studies regarding the synergies which lie in between educational psychology and cognitive neurosciences; furthermore in the following paper a significant emphasis will be attributed to the discovery of the mirror neurons as the basis of empathy and to the relative implications concerning the educational, social and clinical fields.
