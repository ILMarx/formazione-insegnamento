Metadata (EN)

Title: The development of transformative paradigms in the meeting between universities and people held

Abstract: Education and high education in prison are one of the most important ele-ments of emancipation and distancing from the logic of these systems. The university in prison, involved at multiple levels, can guaranteeing access to a right and contribute to developing paradigms capable of overcoming the limits of the current penitentiary approach, restoring dignity, choice and a perspective on the future also to people held.
