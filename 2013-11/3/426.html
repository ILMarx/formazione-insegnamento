Title: Pedagogical observation to know childhood within a kindergarten.Structuring of an instrument

Abstract: Research faces a possible analysis of pedagogical thought, starting from Montessorian suggestions, concerning the relationship between the effectiveness of the action and promotion of autonomy, in an educational institution, the nursery school, aimed at building a systematic dossier tool, of a clear pedagogical brand, capable of grasping the development of the child, in the age group between 336 MESS, and orient them in the maturation direction.
