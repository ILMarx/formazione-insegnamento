Metadata (EN)

Title: Education for entrepreneurship in cooperative contexts

Abstract: This contribution illustrates a cooperative laboratory in the technical training between school and work experience aimed at solving the problems that students have when they cross the boundaries.This laboratory is built on a socio -cultural model and brings together students, teachers and work tutors to find shared solutions.The collective agency mobilized during the discussion qualifies the European key competence for apprenticeships relating to the sense of initiative and entrepreneurship: students find shared solutions and put them in being during an expansive learning cycle that generates innovation. Title: L’educazione all’imprenditorialità in contesti cooperativi

Abstract: Il presente contributo illustra un laboratorio cooperativo nella formazione tecnica tra scuola ed esperienza lavorativa volto alla risoluzione dei problemi che gli studenti hanno quando attraversano i confini. Tale laboratorio è costruito su un modello socioculturale e riunisce studenti, insegnanti e tutor lavorativi per trovare soluzioni condivise.
