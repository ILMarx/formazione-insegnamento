Title: From subsoil to freedom

Abstract: The subsoil represents the state of solitude that a person and/or a community can live, being isolated and outside the cooperative contexts.In this situation there are the Romanì populations residing in the Roma camps, therefore it is believed to be important to reflect on a pedagogical and convivial proposal to overcome the subsoil and land on freedom.
