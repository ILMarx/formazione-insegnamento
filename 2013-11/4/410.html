Metadata (EN)

Title: Temporality and narrativity: for a enhancement of the places of education

Abstract: The elements of narrativity and temporality allow to outline the specificity of life environments intended as educational places capable of digesting opportunities for learning, growth and development of the self.The narrativity, understood as the encounter and sharing of stories, opens the gaze towards the perspective of reciprocity and co-evolution, temporality guarantees the continuity of the experiences experienced in the multiple places of life (between past and present in order to build personal perspective future perspective).
