Title: For intercultural teaching and a philosophy of integration between school and health.Case Studio: the cooperation between the University of Bari "Aldo Moro", the Order of Medici of Bari and the "De Lilla" professional institute of Conversano (Bari)

Abstract: This contribution deals with an experience of intercultural teaching that involved, with a view to an integrated training system, school, university and order of doctors, with the aim of promoting a research-action experience, which participated in pupils of a secondary school emitted of general medicine, aimed at testing the degree of health integration of immigrants in the Italian social context.
