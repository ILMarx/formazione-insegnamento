Metadata (EN)

Title: Reason Responsible Teaching of Philosophy and Educational Perspective

Abstract: The actual cultural climate (in which the themes of the crisis of rationalityand deconstruction of the subject dominate) profoundly affects the teachingof Philosophy in school, which today presents difficult issues and interestingferments. Analysing those elements the essay reflects on the way Philosophyshould be taught seeing it as an opportunity to develop extremelyeffective way of teaching rationality. It tries to identify the dimensions andthe conditions giving much importance to the educational responsibilityand the teaching competence.
