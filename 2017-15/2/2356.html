Metadata (EN)

Title: Appraisal and Collaboration for Teachers’ training: beyond the common sense’s oxymoron

Abstract: In this contribution we will try to read the appraisal as a support in reflectiveteachers’ training path; in a school and community dimensions, we willconsider it as incentive to sharing practices for peer review process orientedto teachers professional development and school improvement. We willconduct this analysis form an historical perspective from the Autonomy tothe Buona Scuola.
