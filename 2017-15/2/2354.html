Metadata (EN)

Title: For a new pedagogical humanism. Between existential void and aspiration to fullness

Abstract: This work is an excursus about the nodal questions of pedagogical theoresisconcerning the idea of humanism and of the human being, consideringtheories that have strongly contributed to the discussion on these themes,specifically the existential analysis of V.E. Frankl and its pedagogical applications.Moreover, there are hints from various researchers on the idea offreedom related to happiness, with particular reference to issues related tothe formation of moral personality. The work ends with a proposal-provocationthat deals about the problematic joints of contemporary educationwith a reference to its meaning and its foundation.
