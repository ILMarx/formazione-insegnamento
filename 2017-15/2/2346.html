Title: Teacher self-assessment. A path for professional growth

Abstract: The study analyzes which strategies and conditions need to trigger an selfassessment on the teacher’s profession. The main theoretical references revolvearound three core category: the theories of self-assessment, the functionof self-evaluating processes for and with teachers, the teachers’s professionaldevelopment. The ability to self-assess their didactic performanceseems to influence and modify behavior, perception of self and thinkingabout teaching practices, and also to support professional growth.
