Title: Online training events for teachers’ professional development: CLIL and technologies in a Learning Event promoted by eTwinning

Abstract: The paper aims at describing an online training initiative (Learning Event)promoted by eTwinning focused on CLIL (Content and Language IntegratedLearning) and on ICT.After a brief overview of the theoretical and methodological framework underlying the two “souls” of the initiative, the paper will try to depict theprocesses of discovering, learning, experimentation and socialization activatedin the participants, directly referring to their comments and remarkscollected through the initial and final questionnaire.Examples of materials produced during the course and excerpts of theteachers’ interactions in the forum and on the blog will help understandhow training episodes such this one may enhance teachers’ professionaldevelopment and improve teaching practices, also considering the latestReform Law (Law 107/2015) and the recently approved National TeacherTraining Plan (2016-2019).
