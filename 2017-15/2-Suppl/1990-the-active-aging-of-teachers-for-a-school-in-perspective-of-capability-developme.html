Metadata (EN)

Title: The active aging of teachers for a school in perspective of capability development

Abstract: This article intends to address the issue of professional development ofteachers in a view of active aging for a school in which the drive for innovationof educational practices requires a recursive professional action of discovery,experimentation and reflection in a multi-perspective view of theschool context. It is therefore proposed a reflection on the need to detectthe dynamics of correlation between possession of Innovator discoveryskills (Christensen et al., 2011) and the organizational and relational conditionsto enhance teacher’s development capabilities for improvement ofstudents’ agency.
