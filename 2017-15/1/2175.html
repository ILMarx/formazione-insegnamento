Metadata (EN)

Title: The new system of Higher Technical Education and Training in Italy

Abstract: The analysis focuses on positive and negative aspects related to the newsystem of Technical Higher Institutes (Istituti Tecnici Superiori: ITSs) that introduced in Italy the third level of Vocational Education and Training. In thefirst part the study provides an updated overview of the legal framework forITSs and some key quantitative data on the sector. Subsequently, the analysistakes into consideration the findings of a recent research conducted byAssociazione TreEllle and Fondazione Rocca. On the basis of interviewswith a panel of key informants, findings highlight main critical issues concerningthe new ITS implementation process.
