Metadata (EN)

Title: Philosophy for Children as inclusive community of inquiry

Abstract: This paper aims to highlight the main characteristics of the Community ofInquiry Philosophy for Children (P4C). P4C is an educational movement anda curriculum for the education of critical, creative, and caring thinking thatadopts dialogical methodologies. The main purpose of this paper is to underlinethe potentialities of P4C and the context of the Community of Inquiryin an inclusive perspective. Starting from a review of Italian and internationalliterature, it will be emphasized how the community of inquiry canbe considered an inclusive community.
