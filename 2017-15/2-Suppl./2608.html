Metadata (EN)

Title: Educational gardens, outdoor school innovation areas

Abstract: The environmental crisis requires to rethink the relationship between humanbeings and the natural world. This relationship needs to be revisedthrough a pedagogical reflection, for finding new educational strategiesable to take care of the environmental question. Starting from the analysisof some educational experiences based on the outdoor horticulture, thetext offers a reflection about some possible ways of re-modeling the usualtraining-spaces, looking at the promotion of multi-level activities linked tothe ecological education.
