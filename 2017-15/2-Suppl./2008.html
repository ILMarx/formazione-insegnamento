Metadata (EN)

Title: Teacher education and inclusive didactics: listening, relationship and participation at school

Abstract: The article describes an experience of teacher education that aimed to improvethe ability to guide a class group in its complexity. This is achievedthrough the recognition and observation of specific difficulties of singlepupils or subsets of the group. It also deals with the ability to design strategiesapt to prevent educational failure and limit causes of school discomfort.It is not possible to have inclusive schools without improving teacherprofessionalism.
