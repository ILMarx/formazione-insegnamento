Metadata (EN)

Title: Women and school leadership. New prospects for educational leadership

Abstract: The essay is about the issue of school leadership feminisation in Italy, a phenomenon of recent onset, still little studied and poorly available in scientificliterature. Looking at studies on female leadership and Diversity Management,the intent is to discuss the possible transformations concerning educationalleadership, generated by the increased number of female leaders,in order to promote educational research projects that could enhanceprocesses of educational innovation through the empowerment of differences.
