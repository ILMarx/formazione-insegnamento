Metadata (EN)

Title: Gathering around a table: food for the body, mind and soul

Abstract: This article comes from some thoughts on how the food is to be considered“nourishment” for the whole Person . It is considered the man in his totality,uniqueness, mind, body, soul of a “biography “ not only written but savoredand sometimes poorly digested in what is the “menu of life.” Ananalysis from the perspective of “ exquisite food “ from where comes out aframed picture of the potentiality and limits of the man in his surroundings.

Title: Ri-trovarsi a tavola: aliment-azione per corpo, mente e anima

Abstract: Questo articolo nasce da alcune riflessioni su come il cibo sia da considerarsi“alimento” per l’intera Persona.
