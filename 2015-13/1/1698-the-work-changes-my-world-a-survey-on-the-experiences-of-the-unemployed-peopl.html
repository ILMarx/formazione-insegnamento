Metadata (EN)

Title: “The work changes my world”: a survey on the experiences of the unemployed people with disabilities over 40 in Lombardy

Abstract: According to the literature, people over the age of 40 are at risk of being excludedfrom the labour market. The situation of those who are in possessionof a disability certificate is particularly critical: they could (should beable to?) take advantage of their right to be employed within organizations,but find themselves without a job. Through in-depth interviews we collectedthe experiences of twenty disabled workers who had been unemployedfor at least two years, and went to the Bergamo employment center to renewtheir enrollment on the unemployment register. The interviews wereanalyzed using a phenomenological approach and the results show that thisexperience has four key aspects: personal identity, the desire e to be moreuseful to the company, personal and family balance, and lack of faith in thefuture.
