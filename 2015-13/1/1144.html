Metadata (EN)

Title: Evaluating interventions of socio-educational policies: between empiricism and hermeneutics

Abstract: This article describes the methodology used for the evaluation programme linked to the Centri-2you intervention of socio-educational policy. Then it points out how intervention’s results could be socially represented through an analysis of ecological data. Such analysis is built by applying a hermeneutic approach.
