Metadata (EN)

Title: Intercultural training of teachers and educators

Abstract: After drawing an updated overview of the presence of immigrants in theItalian society and of non-national students in the school system, the contributionfocuses on the issue of intercultural training of teachers and educatorswith particular reference to continuing education. The contribution, therefore, describes some of the main intercultural strategies for teachers training.
