Metadata (EN)

Title: Educational Crisis and Scientific Design in Education

Abstract: The paper examines two types of educational crisis. The first type crisis as a transition in the ethicality of a social community. And the crisis of the second type as a fading of ethicality. Pedagogical responses to these types of crises are also examined.

Keywords: Crisis; Education; Ethicality; Hegemony; Morality
